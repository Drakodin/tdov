### BACKGROUND WITH MORE DETAILS

My cover takes the original music (since I thought it honestly fit better than anything I could muster), but changes the target to the current president of the US, in a tongue-in-cheek way, but also based on observations from the first 2 months.

**None of this is monetized. It's a joking cover of an old propaganda song that I thought was kind of funny.**
